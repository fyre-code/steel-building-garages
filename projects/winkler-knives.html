New Project Spotlight:
Custom Steel Structure for Winkler Knives

We’re proud to announce a new custom steel building project for Winkler Knives, one of America’s premier blade manufacturers known for their craftsmanship, legacy, and rugged performance. Designed to meet the unique operational needs of their team, this new facility will offer durability, versatility, and secure space for production and expansion.

Key Specs

Building Dimensions:

  • Width: 60′
  • Length: 61′ (with a 60′ frame length under the 61′ roof span)
  • Height: 14′ leg height

Framing: Upgraded 2×3 steel tubing

Metal Sheeting: 29-gauge vertical metal panels

Insulation: Vapor barrier to control moisture and improve interior conditions

Color Scheme: A cohesive, all-brown finish for roof, trim, sidewalls, and endwalls

Functional Design Features

  • Four 12′ x 12′ roll-up bays positioned along the front wall, allowing ease of access for equipment and materials
  • Two walk-in access doors for personnel entry
  • Certified 105-130 MPH / 25 PSF for added stability and code compliance
  • Interior Partition Wall dividing the structure lengthwise into two operational areas
  • Stem Wall Foundation: A portion of the building will be installed on a pre-poured stem wall foundation, allowing for added wall height and framing precision
