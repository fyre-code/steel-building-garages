Who We Are

Who We Are

Steel Building Garages positions itself as a manufacturer-direct supplier specializing in large-scale custom steel structures. We are part of US Building Group, giving us 40 years of factory experience to every project we take on.

We serve commercial, agricultural, industrial, and storage sectors — shipping kits across the nation and providing full installation in select states.

Our mission is simple: connect customers directly with the manufacturer to eliminate middlemen, reduce costs, improve communication, and deliver stronger, more customized buildings.

Why Buy Direct From a Manufacturer?

Better Pricing

Factory-direct sales eliminate dealer markups entirely. You get the same high-quality steel building for thousands less than you'd pay through a dealer network.
